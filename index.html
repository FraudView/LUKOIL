Fraud Alert - LUKOIL

Russia is one of the world's largest exporters of petroleum products, and the vast majority of transactions are made by major Russian companies on a long-term basis with their regular partners.

Be aware of fraudulent schemes that may affect potential buyers of petroleum products. Many companies are looking to source petroleum products (Jet A1, fuel oil M-100, diesel D6 and others among them) from Russia.

There are many companies that claim to be willing to sell Russian-made products in large volumes on a long-term basis. Unfortunately, some of them are fake (fraudulent) companies using forged documents and/or letterheads of the big Russian oil companies. corporations (e.g. Rosneft, Lukoil, Gazprom, etc.).

Usually the fraudulent seller claims to be able to supply petroleum products in large volumes, subject to advance payment of a significant amount and/or some "obligatory" fees (such as export licence fee, title transfer fee, registration fee, etc.). Ð”.).
